  • State of Markov process is dependent on only the most previous state.

  • In a hidden Markov model, the states are not directly observable.

  • The hidden states emit the values of observables based on some distribution.

  • These observables can be used to predict the most likely sequence of hidden states using the Viterbi algorithm.
