  • In this work, HMMs with Gaussian emissions were used to model the switching behavior of the flagellar motor.

  • Rather than multinomial emissions, the emissions can have a range of continuous values (and can overlap).

  • The rotational velocity was used as the observable and run or tumble as the hidden state.

  • HMMs were fit to the pre-silver data and used to predict the states of the post-silver data.
