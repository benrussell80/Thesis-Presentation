  • E. coli Strain: K12-Δhns pHNS-mEos3.2c1

  • Grown overnight in Luria Broth (LB) at 37°C, 250RPM in a shaking incubator

  • Diluted 5000:1 the next day and regrown at 32°C, 250RPM

  • Regrown to OD600=0.3, after which time silver nitrate was added to the liquid culture.

  • Samples were then imaged.
