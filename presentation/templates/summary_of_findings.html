Silver ions slowed E. coli cells, and caused their trajectories to be more curved.

This changed behavior is likely explained by the stalling of the bacterial motor as seen from the hidden Markov modeling.

These effects are likely not due to cell death as the population of cells retained superdiffusivity, and PI staining results were similar to the controls.
